Valletta is the capital of Malta, on the northeast coast of the island of Malta.

BIRKIRKARA

Birkirkara is a city in the central region of Malta.

Birkirkara means "cold water" or "running water". This is attributed to the valley in the town.

St Helen's Basilica, housing Malta's largest church bell.

Area: 2.7 km²
